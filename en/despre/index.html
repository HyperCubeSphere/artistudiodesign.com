About us

Furniture built for the way you live.

We believe in personalised design, precise execution, and transparent communication. From sketch to install, we take care of everything so your space works the way you do.

Our story

Passion for detail. Respect for the space.

ArtiStudio

Arti Studio was born from a wish to make bespoke furniture more accessible, better thought-out, and longer-lasting.

We work with materials we’d use in our own homes, with craftsmen we’ve known for years, and with clients we treat as partners.

Every project is a conversation: we listen, propose, refine, deliver. Nothing more complicated than that.
